Our Services

We Fix All Types of Furniture

From a scratched coffee table to a family heirloom, our team handles repairs of every kind.

🪚
Scratch & Dent Repair
From hairline scratches to deep gouges, we blend repairs invisibly — matched to your piece's original finish.
🏺
Antique Restoration
Heirloom pieces deserve care. We use appropriate techniques and materials to honor the original craftsmanship.
🔩
Structural Repair
Wobbly chairs and loose table legs get full disassembly, old glue removal, and precision regluing.
🛋️
Upholstery Repair
Torn fabric, damaged foam, broken frames — we restore sofas, chairs, and cushions to like-new condition.
✨
Refinishing & Polishing
Strip decades of grime and old varnish. Apply a hand-rubbed oil, lacquer, or shellac finish tailored to your taste.
🪑
Leather Restoration
Cracked, faded, or torn leather furniture gets cleaned, conditioned, and color-matched back to its former glory.
