Our Services

We Fix All Types
of Furniture

From a wobbly dining chair to a family heirloom — if it's wood furniture, we can fix it. All work done in your home.

🪚
Scratch & Dent Repair
Invisible blending using hand-mixed stains matched exactly to your piece's original finish. No patches, no traces.
🏺
Antique Restoration
We treat every heirloom with reverence — using period-appropriate techniques that preserve character and value.
🔩
Structural Repair
Full disassembly, old glue removal, and precision regluing. Wobbly chairs and loose legs won't last long with us.
✨
Refinishing & Polishing
Strip decades of wear and apply a hand-rubbed oil, lacquer, or shellac finish suited to your piece.
